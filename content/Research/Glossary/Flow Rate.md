The volume of fluid passing through a cross-section per unit time.

## Units
- **Metric**: Litres per second (L/s), cubic meters per hour (m³/hr)
- **US**: Gallons per minute (GPM), cubic feet per second (CFS)

## Equations
$$Q = A \times v$$

Where:
- **Q** = flow rate
- **A** = cross-sectional area
- **v** = average velocity
