The height of a liquid column that corresponds to a particular [[pressure]] exerted by the liquid column on the base of its container.
## Equations

$h = \frac{p}{{\rho}g}$

Where:
- h = Pressure Head
- p = Fluid Pressure
- ρ = Fluid Density
- g = Gravitational Acceleration
