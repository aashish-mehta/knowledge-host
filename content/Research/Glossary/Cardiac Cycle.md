The sequence of events that occur in the [[Human Heart|heart]] during one complete heartbeat.

Consists of two phases: [[Diastole]] and [[Systole]].

- Maintains continuous blood flow through the heart and circulatory system.
- Controls blood pressure changes during each heartbeat.
- Coordinates the contraction and relaxation of atria and ventricles for efficient pumping.
