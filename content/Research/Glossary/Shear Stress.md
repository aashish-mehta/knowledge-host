The force per unit area acting parallel to a surface, caused by fluid [[Viscosity]] and velocity gradients. It is the resistance to flow in viscous fluids.

## Mathematical Expression
$$\tau_s = \mu \frac{d\gamma}{dt}$$

Where:
- $\tau_s$ = shear stress
- **μ** = dynamic [[Viscosity]]
- $\frac{d\gamma}{dt}$ = shear strain rate

## Notes
- **Higher viscosity** → higher shear stress
- **Steeper velocity gradient** → higher shear stress
