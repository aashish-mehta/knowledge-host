A **Left Ventricular Assist Device (LVAD)** is a type of [[Ventricular Assist Device]] that helps the **[[Left Ventricle]]** pump blood to the rest of the body.

## Notes

- Supports the **left side** of the [[Human Heart|heart]], which is responsible for **systemic circulation**
- Used in patients with **severe left-sided [[Heart Failure]]**
