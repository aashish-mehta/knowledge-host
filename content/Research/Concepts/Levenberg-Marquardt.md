The Levenberg-Marquardt (LM) algorithm is a popular optimisation technique for training feedforward [[Neural Network|neural networks]]. It blends gradient descent and the Gauss-Newton method to adjust weights efficiently.

**Strengths**:
- Fast convergence
- Effective for small to medium-sized datasets

**Limitations**:
- High memory usage
- Less scalable for large parameter spaces
