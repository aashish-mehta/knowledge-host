# Ventricular Assist Device

A **ventricular assist device (VAD)** is a mechanical pump used to support heart function and blood flow in people with weakened hearts or [[heart failure]]. It helps either the **left**, **right**, or **both** ventricles pump blood.
## Types

- **[[Left Ventricular Assist Device]] (LVAD)** – supports the left ventricle  
- **Right Ventricular Assist Device (RVAD)** – supports the right ventricle
- **Biventricular Assist Device (BiVAD)** – supports both ventricles

## Notes

- Takes blood from a heart chamber and pushes it into the appropriate artery
- Connected externally to a power source and control unit
