**Machine Learning (ML)** is a subfield of [[Artificial Intelligence]] that focuses on building systems that **learn from data** to make predictions or decisions without being explicitly programmed.

Instead of writing rules manually, ML systems **learn patterns** from examples. The more data the model sees, the better it can generalize to new, unseen inputs.
